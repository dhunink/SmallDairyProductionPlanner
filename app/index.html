Small Dairy Production Planner

A visual weekly planning aid for small dairy farms and micro-dairies.

Planning checks

Monday pickupSunday milk production is ready before Monday 08:00.
Wednesday pickupTuesday milk production is ready before Wednesday 08:00.
Labour limitTuesday reaches 8 active hours, equal to the configured limit.
Raw milk ageFriday to Monday tank interval is about 71 hours with a 72-hour limit.
Yoghurt maturationThursday 12:00 to Friday 08:00 gives 20 hours.
